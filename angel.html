Description

Every time Aston chooses to spread his wings, he flies too close to the sun. To what extent can he be an angel without his whole life falling to pieces?

On Aston's 6th Ola Album, "ANGEL", he expands on the work of "lost," returning to original songs. As Aston sings about his idolisation of others, we begin to understand how his own mental well-being is jeopardised, as it dawns on him that no matter how much he tries to be a winged beauty sent from heaven -- he will never be one.
